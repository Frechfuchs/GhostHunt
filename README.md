# GhostHunt

People are intruding your home and you are a ghost!  
Scare them away by possessing objects or make scary sounds with them.

WASD - Move around  
Space - Move up  
Shift - Move down  
E - Use objects  

Developed in 48h (+ some polishing :see_no_evil:)
